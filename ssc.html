SSC CGL

The Staff Selection Commission - Combined Graduate Level (SSC CGL) exam is a national-level exam to recruit candidates for Group B and Group C posts in various ministries, departments, and organizations of the Government of India.

The selection process involves multiple tiers, including computer-based examinations and skill tests, depending on the post.
