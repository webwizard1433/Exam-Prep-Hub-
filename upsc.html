UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE)

The gateway to India's premier civil services.

About the Exam

The Civil Services Examination (CSE) is a nationwide competitive examination in India conducted by the Union Public Service Commission for recruitment to various Civil Services of the Government of India.

Key Services

Successful candidates are placed into prestigious services, including:

  • Indian Administrative Service (IAS)
  • Indian Foreign Service (IFS)
  • Indian Police Service (IPS)

Exam Stages

It is a multi-stage exam designed to test a candidate's aptitude and depth of knowledge, consisting of:

  1. Preliminary Examination: An objective-type screening test.
  2. Main Examination: A written examination with nine papers.
  3. Interview: A personality test for final selection.

At a Glance

Conducting Body: Union Public Service Commission

Frequency: Once a year
